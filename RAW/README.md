# RAW

`RAW/` is the landing zone for external captures before they are processed into the vault.
Configure tools such as Obsidian Web Clipper to save captured pages, articles, YouTube notes,
PDF notes, audio messages, and other source material here.

Treat files in this folder as source evidence:

- Preserve the captured content. Do not rewrite a raw source just to make it prettier.
- Do not store secrets, credentials, regulated records, or private material in the public template.
- In a private vault, review audience and sensitivity before committing raw captures to Git.
- Process useful captures into `shared/knowledge/` using
  `business/runbooks/process-raw-captures.md`.

`RAW/` is like an inbox for sources. `business/inbox/` remains the inbox for operational work,
tasks, decisions, meeting notes, and business inputs that are not source captures.
