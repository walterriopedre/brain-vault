# Customization Guide

Brain Vault is meant to be adapted. Start small, then customize based on repeated real use.

---

## Step 1: Choose A Mode

Pick one:

- Personal.
- Business.
- Mixed with clear personal, business, and shared boundaries.

Update `AGENTS.md` in your private clone to match that choice.

---

## Step 2: Keep Or Remove Optional Areas

Keep folders that serve your workflow. Archive or ignore folders you do not use.

Do not move existing root folders until you understand what tools or links depend on them.

---

## Step 3: Customize Templates

Copy templates from `templates/` and adjust them for your use.

Good first customizations:

- Rename fields to match your language.
- Add owner, due date, or review date fields.
- Remove sections you never fill out.
- Add safety notes for sensitive workflows.

---

## Step 4: Customize AI Rules

Edit `AGENTS.md` first. If using Claude Code, also update `CLAUDE.md` and `_claude/config/`.

Useful additions:

- Session start scan.
- Session end ritual.
- Sensitive data rules.
- Systems of record.
- Preferred folder paths.
- Required review steps.

---

## Step 5: Add Examples Carefully

Examples should be fake unless your private vault is approved for real data.

Good fake example names:

- Example Person.
- Example Company.
- Sample Project.
- Fictional Launch Brief.

Do not use real customer or employee names as examples.
